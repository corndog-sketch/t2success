Alex Corneliusen started Tutor 2 Success with a simple belief: every student in the Riverina deserves access to quality, personalised tutoring from someone who genuinely knows and cares about this community.

Having lived and studied in the Riverina region, Alex brings firsthand understanding of Wagga Wagga students and the NSW curriculum. That local knowledge shapes everything about how Tutor 2 Success is run.

Genuinely personalised, properly structured, and fairly priced. We are personally involved in every part of it. Every enquiry, every tutor match, every session note review.

"To give Wagga Wagga and Riverina students access to quality, personalised tutoring from people who actually understand the local experience, at prices that work for regional families."

Alex works directly with every student, personally handling every session, every enquiry, and every follow-up. If you ever have a question or concern, Alex is your point of contact.

Our History

THE Tutor 2 Success STORY

Tutor 2 Success is not a brand new venture. It has roots, local experience, and a clear reason for coming back.

2020

Tutor 2 Success Founded

Alex Corneliusen launched Tutor 2 Success in Wagga Wagga, initially focused on tutoring for local secondary school students. The business grew organically through word of mouth in the local school community.

2020 to 2022

Original Operation

Tutor 2 Success operated for two years as a Wagga Wagga-based tutoring service. Sessions were delivered in-person, building a track record and local reputation in the community.

2022

Temporary Pause

Tutor 2 Success paused operations as Alex's circumstances changed. The brand, experience, and local relationships remained, waiting for the right time to return.

2026

The Relaunch

Tutor 2 Success returns as a hybrid in-person and remote tutoring service, expanded to cover K-12 subjects across primary and secondary school. Alex is personally delivering all sessions, with a focus on building strong, long-term relationships with local families.

WHY THE RELAUNCH?

The demand from local families remained strong. The 2026 relaunch gives Tutor 2 Success the opportunity to serve more students with a stronger, broader model.

The relaunch introduces hybrid delivery so students anywhere in the Riverina can access sessions remotely, and a broader subject offering across K-12. Alex personally delivers every session and handles all communication directly with families.

Alex is WWCC-verified and brings years of local tutoring experience across the Riverina. Every student is matched personally based on subject, year level, and learning needs.

How Alex Works

THE TUTOR 2 SUCCESS APPROACH

Every session is delivered personally by Alex. Here is what that means for your family.

✅

WWCC VERIFIED

Alex holds a current, valid Working With Children Check. Documentation is available on request at any time.

🎓

K-12 ACROSS ALL SUBJECTS

Alex tutors across a broad range of K-12 subjects, from primary school English and Maths through to HSC-level English, Science, Business Studies, Legal Studies, and Computing.

LOCAL AND PERSONAL

Alex grew up in the Riverina and understands the local school environment, the NSW curriculum, and the pressures Wagga Wagga students and families face.

SESSION NOTES EVERY TIME
