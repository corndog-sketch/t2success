LOCAL, PERSONAL, AND GENUINELY INVESTED

Alex Corneliusen started Tutor 2 Success out of a conviction that regional students should not have to accept less, less access, less support, or less quality, just because they are not in Sydney or Melbourne.

Having lived and studied in the Riverina region, Alex understands firsthand the pressures that students in Wagga Wagga and surrounds face: fewer resources, less peer competition to push against, and tutoring options that are either overpriced national chains or informal arrangements with questionable consistency.

Tutor 2 Success was built to be something different. Genuinely personalised, properly structured, and fairly priced. We are personally involved in every part of it. Every enquiry, every tutor match, every session note review.

"To give Wagga Wagga and Riverina students access to quality, personalised tutoring from people who actually understand the local experience, at prices that work for regional families."

We work directly with students on a one-on-one basis and personally select and oversee every university tutor who delivers sessions on behalf of Tutor 2 Success. If you ever have a question or concern, we are here.

Our History

THE Tutor 2 Success STORY

Tutor 2 Success is not a brand new venture. It has roots, local experience, and a clear reason for coming back.

2020

Tutor 2 Success Founded

Alex Corneliusen launched Tutor 2 Success in Wagga Wagga, initially focused on tutoring for local secondary school students. The business grew organically through word of mouth in the local school community.

2020 to 2022

Original Operation

Tutor 2 Success operated for two years as a Wagga Wagga-based tutoring service. Sessions were delivered in-person, building a track record and local reputation in the community.

2022

Temporary Pause

Tutor 2 Success paused operations as Alex's circumstances changed. The brand, experience, and local relationships remained, waiting for the right time to return.

2026

The Relaunch

Tutor 2 Success returns as a hybrid in-person and remote tutoring service, expanded to cover K-12 subjects across primary and secondary school. The relaunch adds a university tutor network to extend capacity while maintaining quality control, with local high-achieving university students recruited and onboarded directly by Alex.

WHY THE RELAUNCH?

The demand never went away. Wagga Wagga students still face the same challenges: limited access to specialist tutoring, big-city pricing from national chains, and a shortage of tutors who actually understand the local context.

The 2026 relaunch introduces a stronger model: hybrid delivery so regional students anywhere in the Riverina can access sessions remotely, a broader subject offering across K-12, and a structured university tutor network to provide more capacity without sacrificing quality.

Every tutor is personally selected by Alex, WWCC-verified, and briefed on the Tutor 2 Success approach. They are not anonymous contractors. They are high-achieving university students from the local community who know and care about the students they teach.

The Tutor Network

Tutor 2 Success draws on a network of high-achieving local university students who are recruited, screened, and personally onboarded by Alex. This is not a marketplace. It is a curated team where Alex knows every tutor and stands behind every session they deliver.

Our Tutors

THE Tutor 2 Success TUTOR STANDARD

Whether you are working with Alex directly or with one of our university tutors, the standard does not drop. Every Tutor 2 Success tutor meets the same requirements.

WWCC VERIFIED

LOCAL CONTEXT

PERSONALLY SELECTED
