Simple and Straightforward

HOW IT WORKS

From your first enquiry to your student's first session. Here is exactly what to expect when you come to Tutor 2 Success. No surprises, no complicated onboarding. Alex handles it all personally.

1

ENQUIRE VIA THE WEBSITE

Fill out the short contact form on our Contact page or send us an email directly. Tell us your student's year level, subject, and what you are hoping to work on. No forms to print, no lengthy sign-up process.

📋 We read every enquiry personally and typically respond within 24 hours to confirm receipt and arrange a time to chat.
2

FREE CHAT WITH US

We will have a brief conversation with you by phone, email, or video to understand your student's specific needs, the subjects they need support in, and the outcomes you are aiming for. This is completely free with no obligation.

We will also answer any questions you have about how sessions run and confirm the rate before anyone commits to anything.

💬 This chat usually takes 10 to 15 minutes. It helps ensure Tutor 2 Success is the right fit before you spend a cent.
3

BOOK YOUR TRIAL SESSION

Ready to go? Book your first session, which is 50% off as our introductory offer. This is your chance to experience Tutor 2 Success firsthand and see whether it is the right fit before committing to ongoing sessions.

⭐ First session is 50% off the standard rate. No conditions, no catch. There is no lock-in contract.
4

WE MATCH YOUR STUDENT

Based on your student's subject, year level, learning style, and availability, we personally match them to the right tutor. That may be Alex directly, or one of our high-achieving university tutors from the local area. Every tutor holds a current Working With Children Check and is hand-picked for their subject knowledge.

✅ If the match does not feel right after the trial, just let us know. We will reassess and find a better fit.
5

ONGOING SESSIONS

Once you are happy with the setup, Alex establishes a regular schedule that suits your student and family. Sessions are managed through our booking system for easy scheduling, invoicing, and session notes.

After each session, a written summary is shared with the family covering what was covered, homework set, and any areas to focus on next time. Alex stays in touch throughout and is always available if you have a question.

📅 Need to reschedule? Just give Alex reasonable notice. We work with families to keep sessions consistent without making it stressful.
Quick Answers

COMMON QUESTIONS

A few things families often ask at this stage.

Is there a minimum number of sessions?

No. There is no lock-in contract and no minimum commitment. Most families find a regular weekly schedule delivers the best results, but you are never obligated to continue.

What if the trial session is not a good fit?

Just let Alex know. He will have an honest conversation about what would work better, whether that is a different tutor, a different format, or simply that Tutor 2 Success is not the right match. No hard feelings.

Do sessions happen at our home?

Yes. Tutor 2 Success does not have an office. In-person sessions take place at your home or a mutually agreed location in Wagga Wagga. Remote students connect via Zoom from wherever they are.

How do I pay?

Invoices are issued through our session management system. Payment details are provided at confirmation. We accept standard electronic payment methods. No cash required.
