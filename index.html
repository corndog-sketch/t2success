Why Choose Tutor 2 Success

WHAT SETS US APART

We are not a national chain. We are a local tutoring service that knows Wagga Wagga, knows the NSW curriculum, and genuinely cares about your child's progress across every subject.

🏫

BROAD CURRICULUM EXPERTISE

From English essays to Legal Studies, Maths to Computing Technology, our tutors cover the full K-12 NSW curriculum. We are not a one-subject shop.

📋

STRUCTURED SESSIONS

Every session follows a plan. Session notes are kept after each lesson so students, parents, and tutors stay aligned on progress and next steps.

🏠

WE COME TO YOU

We do not have an office. In-person sessions happen at your home or a mutually agreed location in Wagga Wagga. Remote students connect via Zoom with a shared digital whiteboard.

👥

SMALL GROUP OPTION

Group sessions are capped at three students from the same year level and subject, keeping them genuinely small and focused, not a classroom in disguise.

🎓

HIGH-ACHIEVING UNI TUTORS

All sub-tutors are high-achieving local university students, personally selected by Alex for their subject knowledge and communication ability. Every one holds a current Working With Children Check.

✉

ALWAYS IN TOUCH

We handle every enquiry personally and stay in contact throughout your time with Tutor 2 Success. You are never passed off to an anonymous system. You always have a real person to speak to.

A Personal Note

FROM ALEX

I started Tutor 2 Success because I grew up in regional NSW and watched students fall behind, not because they were not capable, but because quality support was not accessible where they lived.

Tutor 2 Success is not a platform where you browse tutors and hope for the best. When you get in touch, you are talking to me directly. I will listen, figure out the right fit for your student, and stay in the loop throughout. If something is not working, you come back to me and we sort it out together.

That is the business I want to run. Local, personal, and genuinely invested in the students we work with.
