Session Formats

HOW WE TUTOR

Every student is different. We offer four session formats to suit different learning styles, budgets, and goals.

👤

ONE-ON-ONE TUTORING

The core Tutor 2 Success experience. A single student working with a tutor, entirely focused on their specific needs, gaps, and goals. Sessions are tailored to the individual, not a pre-packaged program.

Available with Alex directly, or with one of our screened university tutors based on availability and subject fit. We personally match every student.

All K-12 subjects Primary to HSC In-person or remote
👥

SMALL GROUP SESSIONS

Group sessions are capped at a maximum of three students from the same year level and subject. This keeps the group genuinely small, so everyone still gets attention, questions answered, and personal feedback.

Group sessions are priced at a lower per-student rate than individual sessions, making them a great option for friends or students at the same school.

Max 3 students Same year and subject Lower per-student rate
🏥

HOLIDAY WORKSHOPS

Intensive sessions run during school holiday periods, designed to consolidate term content, tackle weak areas, or get ahead before exams. Available in group format at a per-student rate.

Workshop dates are published each term. Enquire via the contact form to register your interest.

School holidays Exam prep focus Group rate
📋

STUDY TIMETABLE AND PLANNING

Structured support to help students build a realistic and effective study schedule. We work with the student to map out their upcoming commitments, identify priority topics, and plan their revision so nothing is left to the last minute.

Can be done as a standalone session or integrated into regular tutoring.

All year levels Exam preparation Trial period planning
How We Deliver

WE COME TO YOU OR REMOTE

Tutor 2 Success does not have a fixed office. In-person sessions happen at your home or a mutually agreed location. Remote students connect via Zoom from anywhere in regional NSW.

🏠

IN-PERSON SESSIONS

We come to you. In-person sessions take place at your home or a mutually agreed location in Wagga Wagga. No need to travel.

  • Direct, hands-on support, great for working through problems step by step
  • Immediate feedback on written working and exam technique
  • Builds a strong tutor-student rapport for sustained progress
  • Available for students in and around Wagga Wagga
💻

REMOTE SESSIONS

Delivered via Zoom or Google Meet with a shared digital whiteboard, so students can see working, mark up problems, and collaborate in real time, just as they would in person.

  • Full digital whiteboard, tutors and students write, draw, and solve together
  • Sessions recorded on request for student review
  • Available to students anywhere in regional NSW
  • Same price as in-person, no remote surcharge

Is This Right For You?

WHO Tutor 2 Success SUITS

We work best with students who are motivated to improve and families who want clear, honest communication about progress.

THE STUDENT FALLING BEHIND

Whether it is English essays, algebra, or Legal Studies, we diagnose the gap and work through it systematically, not just reteach the same thing in the same way.

THE STUDENT AIMING HIGH

Students targeting Band 6 results or a high ATAR need more than classroom instruction. We go deeper into the subject, work on harder problems, and build the exam technique that separates top marks from good marks.

THE STUDENT BUILDING CONFIDENCE

Some students know more than they think. They just lack confidence. We focus on building a solid foundation, reinforcing what they already know, and gradually tackling harder content as their confidence grows.

REGIONAL STUDENTS

Distance should not limit access to quality tutoring. Our remote sessions are designed to be just as effective as in-person, and we understand the pressures facing students in regional NSW.

PARENTS WANTING VISIBILITY

Session notes are kept after every lesson, so you will always know what was covered, what homework was set, and how your child is tracking. We are always available if you have a question between sessions.

FAMILIES ON A BUDGET

We price below the big tutoring chains deliberately. The 50%-off trial session means you can test the fit before committing, with no lock-in contract required.

FIND THE RIGHT SESSION FORMAT

Not sure which service suits your student? Get in touch and we will have a free chat to recommend the right fit.
