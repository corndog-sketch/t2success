BECOME A TUTOR

We are looking for high-achieving local university students who want to make a real difference for Wagga Wagga students. Flexible hours, local students, and a team you can rely on.

What We Are Looking For

THE TUTOR STANDARD

  • Currently enrolled at university in Wagga Wagga or the surrounding region
  • Strong academic results in your chosen subject areas
  • Current Working With Children Check (WWCC) or willingness to obtain one before starting
  • Reliable, punctual, and committed to your students
  • Clear and patient communication with K-12 students
  • Available for at least a few hours per week on a consistent schedule
What You Get
📅
Flexible Scheduling

Sessions are arranged around your university timetable. You set your availability and we match you accordingly.

🏫
Matched to Your Subjects

You only tutor in areas you are confident in. We will work with you to identify the right fit.

🤝
Full Support From Alex

You are not on your own. Alex provides onboarding, ongoing guidance, and is always available if a session throws a curveball.

📋
Simple Session Structure

Clear expectations, session notes format provided, and a consistent approach so every student gets the same quality experience.
